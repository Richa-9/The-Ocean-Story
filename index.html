Our Story

"Where Every Flavor Tells a Seafood Tale"

Our journey began with a simple love for the sea. We believe that the best meals start with the freshest ingredients, which is why we source our seafood directly from trusted local fishermen. Our passion for quality drives us to select only the finest catches each day, ensuring that every dish we serve is bursting with flavor. Join us and taste the difference that dedication and freshness make.

Our Speciality

Chingri Malai Curry

Chingri Malai curry is a traditional Indian curry originating from the Bengal region. It’s made with a base of coconut milk and prawns that are seasoned with garam masala spices and fried in ghee or mustard oil with onions, hot chili peppers, garlic-ginger paste, and turmeric.
